Top Crypto Price Tracking Tools

Here are some of the most reliable tools to track real-time cryptocurrency prices, market movements, and trading volumes:

  • CoinGecko – Real-time prices, volume, market cap, and fundamental crypto metrics. Free API and no login required.
  • CoinMarketCap – One of the oldest price trackers, now owned by Binance. Offers historical data and exchange volumes.
  • TradingView – For advanced charting and price analysis. Supports multiple crypto pairs and technical indicators.
  • DexScreener – Best for real-time decentralized exchange (DEX) token tracking, especially for new launches.
  • Coin360 – A visual heatmap of the crypto market with price and volume metrics.
  • Nomics – Provides exchange transparency ratings and clean historical data.

These tools help you keep track of the market in real time and make better trading or investment decisions.
