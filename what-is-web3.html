What is Web3?

Web3 is the next evolution of the internet — where users control their data, identity, and assets using blockchain technology.

🌐 Web1 → Web2 → Web3

  • Web1: Read-only (1990s websites)
  • Web2: Read + write (social media, apps — but controlled by big tech)
  • Web3: Read + write + own (powered by blockchain & crypto)

💡 Key Web3 Features:

  • ✅ You own your identity (via crypto wallets)
  • ✅ You own your data (no central server)
  • ✅ You can earn while you use the platform (play-to-earn, social tokens)

🧠 Real-World Analogy:

Web2 is like Facebook — you’re a user, but they control everything. Web3 is like using a blockchain app — you log in with your wallet, and you’re in charge.

🔧 Web3 Examples:

  • Metamask, Phantom (login tool for Web3 apps)
  • Uniswap,Jupiter (decentralized exchange)
  • Lens Protocol (Web3 social media)
  • Decentraland (Web3 virtual worlds)

Web3 shifts the power from corporations to communities. It’s the internet owned by users, not by platforms.
