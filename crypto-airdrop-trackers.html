Crypto Airdrop Trackers

Crypto airdrops are free distributions of tokens to wallet holders, often used to promote new projects or reward loyal users. Airdrop trackers help you discover upcoming or active airdrops so you don’t miss out on free crypto opportunities.

🎯 Why Use Airdrop Trackers?

  • Stay informed about live and upcoming airdrops
  • Filter by blockchain (e.g., Ethereum, Solana, Arbitrum)
  • Track eligibility criteria and claim deadlines
  • Identify potential sybil-resistant and genuine opportunities

📡 Top Airdrop Tracking Tools:

  • Airdrops.io – Popular airdrop aggregator with filters
  • Airdrops.fyi – Clean interface with eligibility status
  • Earndrop – Airdrops + on-chain activity tracking
  • Zealy – Community tasks to earn project rewards
  • Guild.xyz – Token-gated and role-based reward access

🛡️ Caution:

Always verify airdrop links and never share private keys. Use a separate wallet if unsure about a project’s credibility.
