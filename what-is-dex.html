What is a DEX (Decentralized Exchange)?

A DEX, or Decentralized Exchange, is a crypto trading platform that operates without a central authority. Unlike traditional exchanges where a company manages your funds and trades, a DEX lets you trade peer-to-peer directly from your wallet.

Trades happen using smart contracts on blockchains like Ethereum or Solana. No need to sign up or give your identity—just connect your wallet and trade.

Benefits:

  • ✅ No sign-up needed
  • ✅ You control your funds (non-custodial)
  • ✅ Global and borderless

Examples of DEXs:

  • Uniswap – Ethereum-based DEX
  • Raydium – Solana-based DEX
  • BaseSwap – Built on Base network
  • Jupiter – Solana aggregator for best prices

Real-World Analogy:

Imagine going to a local farmer's market (DEX) where you buy vegetables directly from farmers. No middlemen. You pay, they deliver. Quick and direct.
