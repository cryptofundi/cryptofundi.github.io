What is a DEX (Decentralized Exchange)?

A DEX, or Decentralized Exchange, is a crypto trading platform that operates without a central authority. Unlike traditional exchanges where a company manages your funds and trades, a DEX lets you trade peer-to-peer directly from your wallet.
