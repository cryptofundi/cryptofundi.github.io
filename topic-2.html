Top 5 Things to Know Before You Start with Crypto

  1. Crypto is Volatile: Prices can swing wildly. Never invest more than you can afford to lose.
  2. Use Secure Wallets: Choose between hot and cold wallets. Store your private keys safely.
  3. Scams are Everywhere: Always double-check before clicking links or sending crypto.
  4. DYOR – Do Your Own Research: Don’t follow hype blindly. Understand the technology behind projects.
  5. Start Small: Begin with small amounts and learn by doing. Practice using exchanges and wallets safely.
