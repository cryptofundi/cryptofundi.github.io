Top NFT Marketplaces

NFT marketplaces are platforms where creators, collectors, and investors can buy, sell, or trade non-fungible tokens (NFTs). Each marketplace often specializes in certain blockchains or types of NFTs. Here's a look at the most popular ones:

üîπ Ethereum-Based Marketplaces

  • OpenSea: The largest and most well-known NFT marketplace supporting Ethereum, Polygon, and Base.
  • Blur: A trader-focused platform with low fees and bulk-listing tools.
  • Rarible: A creator-centric platform allowing for multi-chain NFT trading.
  • Zora: Focused on digital art and cultural collectibles, especially for independent creators.

üîπ Solana-Based Marketplaces

  • Magic Eden: The leading marketplace for Solana NFTs. Now supports Bitcoin and Ethereum as well.
  • Tensor: A professional-grade trading platform for Solana NFTs.

üîπ Bitcoin NFT Platforms

  • Ordinals Wallet: Allows users to buy and sell NFTs inscribed on Bitcoin using the Ordinals protocol.
  • Magic Eden (Bitcoin Section): Supports Ordinals and now expanding into Bitcoin-based assets.
  • OpenStamp.io: A marketplace for Bitcoin Stamps, a type of NFT permanently embedded into Bitcoin's UTXO set.
  • Gamma: Another Ordinals marketplace for trading Bitcoin NFTs with wallet integration.

üîπ Polygon & Others

  • OpenSea (Polygon): Offers low-cost NFT minting and trading with Ethereum compatibility.
  • Objkt: The largest marketplace on the Tezos blockchain for art-based NFTs.
