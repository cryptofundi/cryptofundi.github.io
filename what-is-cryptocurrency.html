What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital money that is secure, decentralized, and works over the internet without needing banks or governments to control it.

🚀 Key Idea

It’s money you can send, receive, or invest from anywhere — instantly and securely.

🔐 How It Works

  • Uses a public technology called blockchain to record all transactions.
  • No one person, company, or government controls it.
  • Users hold their funds in digital wallets.

💡 Simple Examples

  • Bitcoin (BTC) → Like digital gold. Limited supply. Store of value.
  • Ethereum (ETH) → Like programmable money. Runs smart contracts.

🌍 Why It Matters

  • Fast, cheap cross-border payments
  • Bank the unbanked — anyone with internet can use it
  • Ownership and control stay with the user

In short, cryptocurrency is the future of money — open to everyone, not just banks.
