Top Crypto Tools You Should Know

CoinGecko

A powerful crypto tracking platform that provides real-time price updates, market cap rankings, and analytics on thousands of cryptocurrencies and tokens.

CoinMarketCap

Another top crypto price and market tracking platform. Offers historical data, market cap info, watchlists, and exchange rankings.

DexTools

Essential tool for DeFi users. Lets you analyze and track trading pairs on decentralized exchanges like Uniswap and PancakeSwap in real time.

DexScreener

Great tool to discover trending tokens and new launches on multiple blockchains. Helps with spotting price action and liquidity changes quickly.

Etherscan

Ethereum blockchain explorer. You can view wallet transactions, smart contracts, token info, and track gas prices.

SolanaScan

Blockchain explorer for Solana. Lets you view transactions, wallet balances, NFT transfers, and token data on Solana chain.

Mempool.space

Track Bitcoin transactions in real-time as they enter the mempool. Useful for understanding Bitcoin fees and congestion.

GeckoTerminal

Live DEX trading terminal from CoinGecko. View charts, volume, and trades across DEXs and chains with a trader-friendly interface.

Pump.fun

Launch meme coins on Solana instantly. A gamified platform where anyone can start a token without technical knowledge.

DeFiLlama

Track the total value locked (TVL) in DeFi projects across all blockchains. Ideal for understanding market trends and protocol strength.

Arkham Intelligence

Blockchain analysis platform offering real-time tracking of wallets and smart contracts. Popular for doxxing wallets and understanding on-chain behavior.

TokenSniffer

Analyze newly launched tokens for risk factors. Helps detect scams and unsafe contracts before you invest.

Crypto Tax Calculator

Helps you calculate your tax liability from crypto trades and activities. Supports most countries and exchanges.
