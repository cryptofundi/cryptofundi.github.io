Crypto Risks

Like any financial system, cryptocurrency has its risks. Understanding them helps you make smarter and safer decisions.

‚ö†Ô∏è Top Risks

  • Volatility: Crypto prices can swing wildly, leading to large gains or losses.
  • Security Breaches: Hacks on exchanges and wallets can result in lost funds.
  • Scams: From rug pulls to fake airdrops, fraud is common.
  • Regulatory Risk: Governments may ban or heavily regulate crypto.
  • Loss of Keys: If you lose access to your private keys, your funds are gone forever.

üõ°Ô∏è How to Protect Yourself

  • Use hardware or multi-sig wallets for long-term storage.
  • Do your own research (DYOR) before investing.
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links or connecting unknown wallets.
  • Use regulated exchanges and stay updated on local laws.

Bottom line: Crypto offers high reward but comes with high risk. Learn, stay cautious, and never invest more than you can afford to lose.
