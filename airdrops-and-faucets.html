Airdrops and Faucets

🎁 What Are Airdrops?

Airdrops are free token distributions by blockchain projects to reward early users, promote new projects, or decentralize governance. No purchase is needed, but eligibility criteria may apply.

✅ How to Qualify:

  • Use early-stage dApps (e.g., Uniswap, Arbitrum, LayerZero apps)
  • Hold specific tokens or NFTs in your wallet
  • Join community tasks on platforms like Galxe or Zealy

🚀 Popular Airdrop Trackers:

  • airdrops.io
  • airdrops.live
  • earni.fi (formerly airdrop.fyi)
  • galxe.com (for quests that lead to drops)

🪙 What Are Faucets?

Faucets give you small amounts of crypto (usually testnet tokens) for free. They're commonly used to test dApps or explore networks without financial risk.

🌐 Top Testnet Faucets:

  • QuickNode Faucet Hub – Multi-chain faucets
  • Paradigm Faucet – Ethereum Sepolia & Base testnet
  • Sol Faucet – For Solana devs
  • Testnet Faucet – Multi-network support

💡 Tips for Using Faucets:

  • Always use test wallets for faucets
  • Faucets are rate-limited, so be patient
  • Do not trust faucets asking for private keys

⚠️ Watch Out for Scams

Legit airdrops and faucets will never ask for your private key or seed phrase. Stick to trusted sources and use wallets like MetaMask or Phantom.
