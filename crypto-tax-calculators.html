Crypto Tax Calculators

Crypto tax calculators help you calculate and report taxes on your cryptocurrency transactions, including trading, staking, NFTs, and DeFi activities.

📌 Why You Need a Tax Calculator

  • Track profits, losses, and capital gains accurately
  • Comply with your country’s crypto tax regulations
  • Generate tax-ready reports (e.g., IRS Form 8949, capital gains summary, etc.)
  • Support for multiple wallets, exchanges, and blockchains

💼 Top Crypto Tax Tools

  • Koinly: https://koinly.io — Global tax reporting, portfolio tracking, NFT support
  • CoinTracker: https://cointracker.io — Simple interface, used by Coinbase
  • CoinLedger (formerly CryptoTrader.Tax): https://coinledger.io — Easy reporting for US users
  • Accointing: https://www.accointing.com — Trade analytics + tax reports
  • ZenLedger: https://www.zenledger.io — IRS-compliant reports and audit support

🌍 Country-Specific Tools

  • India: Koinx.com, Koinx supports Indian tax slabs
  • USA: ZenLedger, CoinLedger, and Koinly have IRS-compliant tools
  • UK/EU: Koinly and Accointing offer region-specific tax formats

🛠 How It Works

  1. Connect your wallets and exchanges via API or CSV
  2. Review your transactions: trades, income, staking, NFTs
  3. Generate downloadable tax reports for your region

✅ Pro Tip

Even if your country doesn’t yet regulate crypto taxes, it’s wise to track everything now to avoid compliance issues later.
